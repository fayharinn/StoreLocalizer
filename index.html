App Store & Play Store Localizer

StoreLocalizer is a free, open-source tool that translates your App Store and Google Play listings into 40+ languages with AI. Connect to App Store Connect and the Google Play Console to fetch, translate, and publish localized metadata, screenshots, and pricing.

Features

  • App Store localization — translate App Store Connect metadata and ASO fields.
  • Google Play localization — localize Play Console listings in bulk.
  • .xcstrings translator — translate Xcode String Catalogs locally in your browser.
  • App Store screenshots — generate device-framed, localized screenshots.
  • Subscription pricing — GDP-adjusted in-app purchase price recommendations.

FAQ

Is StoreLocalizer free?

Yes. It is completely free and open-source — you only bring your own AI provider API key.

How many languages are supported?

More than 40 languages for both App Store and Google Play listings.

Does it connect to App Store Connect and Google Play?

Yes. It integrates directly with both so you can fetch, translate, and push localized metadata.
